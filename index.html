Privacy Policy

📅 Odia Calendar (Okal) Effective: May 04, 2026

Odia Calendar (Okal) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Odia Calendar (Okal) does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise GPS location.

The app may collect non-personal information automatically through third-party SDKs, including:

  • App usage data (such as screens viewed or features used)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Google Advertising ID (AD_ID) for advertising and analytics purposes
  • Crash reports and performance data (to improve app stability)

This data is collected only for improving app performance, showing ads, and enhancing the user experience.

2. Permissions Used

Odia Calendar (Okal) requests only the minimum essential permissions required to function properly:

  • INTERNET – To load updates, calendar data, and display advertisements.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – Required to reschedule festival notifications and daily reminders automatically after your device reboots.
  • VIBRATE – To provide haptic feedback and vibrate the device for notification alerts.
  • BILLING – Required to process secure in-app purchases or subscriptions if you choose to upgrade the app.

📁 Storage & Media Access Notice

This app does not request, require, or use persistent storage or media permissions (such as READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, or WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE). If the app ever interacts with media files, it relies strictly on the secure Android System Photo Picker, which provides one-time access without reading device storage, fully complying with Google Play’s Photo and Video Permissions policy.

3. Advertisements and Third-Party Services

Odia Calendar (Okal) displays advertisements provided by third-party ad services (Google AdMob). These services use the Google Advertising ID (AD_ID) to serve relevant ads and perform analytics in compliance with Google Play policies.

We do not directly collect, store, or share this data ourselves. You can manage or reset your ad tracking preferences at any time through your Android device settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads).

4. Data Security

We value your trust and take reasonable measures to protect the app and its data. Since no personal data is collected or stored on our servers, the risk of personal data exposure is mitigated.

5. Children’s Privacy

Odia Calendar (Okal) is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
